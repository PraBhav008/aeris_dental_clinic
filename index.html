What we treat

Six disciplines, one quiet studio.

Every service below is delivered by a dedicated clinician team, in the same building, with shared records — so you're never re-explaining your history.

Preventive Care

Cleanings, sealants and check-ups built around a six-month rhythm, tracked so nothing slips.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Veneers, whitening and bonding planned from a digital smile preview before any work begins.

Orthodontics

Clear aligners and traditional braces, with monthly progress scans included in every plan.

Implants

Single-tooth to full-arch implant work, guided by 3D imaging done on site.

Emergency Care

Same-day slots held daily for pain, breaks and swelling — call ahead, walk in.

Pediatric Dentistry

A separate, softer-paced wing for younger patients, run by our children's specialist.

Why Aeris

Built by clinicians who got tired of rushed ten-minute visits.

Aeris started as a single chair and a belief: patients understand their own mouths better when someone actually takes the time to show them, not just tell them.

Today the studio runs six specialities under one roof, sharing a single patient record so your orthodontist and your hygienist are reading from the same page.

"We plan every treatment around the appointment we'd want for our own family."

05 — APPARATUS

What the room actually contains.

3D imaging (CBCT)

Low-dose cone-beam CT for implant planning and root canal diagnosis — a fraction of the radiation of a hospital scan.

Digital impressions

Intraoral scanning replaces the putty tray for most cases — faster, and considerably more comfortable.

Same-day crowns

Our in-house mill designs and produces ceramic crowns in a single appointment for most posterior cases.

Laser-assisted therapy

Soft-tissue laser for gum contouring and minor procedures, reducing bleeding and recovery time.

06 — PROTOCOL

Sterilization is a procedure, not a gesture.

A

Every instrument passes through a Class B vacuum autoclave and is tracked to the individual patient appointment.

B

Single-use barriers on handpieces, light switches and chair controls are replaced between every patient, not every session.

C

Surgeries are fitted with HEPA filtration and surfaces are wiped with hospital-grade disinfectant between appointments.

12 — QUERIES

Questions, answered plainly.

Do you accept new patients without a referral?

Yes. Most patients register directly; no referral is needed for general or cosmetic treatment. Some orthodontic and surgical referrals may still apply — we'll tell you if yours does.

How soon can I be seen for pain?

We hold same-day emergency slots every day we're open. Call the urgent line above and we'll fit you in, usually within a few hours.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Photo ID, your insurance details if applicable, and a list of any current medications. Arrive ten minutes early for paperwork.

Do you treat anxious or nervous patients?

Regularly. Tell us at booking and we'll allocate longer appointment slots and talk through each step before it happens.

Can children be seen at the studio?

Yes, from around age three. [REPLACE — confirm minimum age policy and paediatric offering]

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?
